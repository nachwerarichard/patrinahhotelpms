Traveler's Guide

When is the Imbalu (Circumcision) Season?
The ceremony happens every even year (2024, 2026, etc.), typically peaking in August. We provide respectful, guided cultural tours to witness this rite of passage.
How far is the hotel from Sipi Falls?
We are located in Mbale City, approximately a 1-hour scenic drive to the Sipi trailhead. We provide daily shuttle services for our guests.
