Elgon Heights Manor is more than a hotel; it is a sanctuary for the discerning traveler. Established to showcase the beauty of Mbale, we provide an international standard of hospitality while remaining deeply rooted in the local traditions of Eastern Uganda.

From the aroma of fresh Bugisu Arabica coffee in the morning to the breathtaking views of Wanale Ridge at sunset, every moment here is curated for excellence.
